Sport Innovation

Sports innovation is an emerging research area linking sport to innovation management and practice. Innovation in sport can be seen in new technologies, equipment and clothing, in new strategies and training, in new consumer products and services, in new media and formats, and in the development of new sports. Although the role of sports innovation is widely recognized in global business and media, less attention has centered on it from an academic approach
